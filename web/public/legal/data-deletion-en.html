Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

This page explains how to delete your account and personal data in the Corio Docs application. In accordance with GDPR and App Store requirements, we provide you with the right to delete your data.

Deletion Methods

1

In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the Corio Docs app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select Account
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion on the confirmation screen
2

Email Deletion Request

Send an email to docs@corioscan.com using the template below:

Subject: Account Deletion Request Hello, I request the deletion of my Corio Docs account and all associated data. My email address: [your registered email address] Thank you.

Timeline

  • In-app deletion: Effective immediately, technical processes completed within 30 days
  • Email deletion request: Processed and completed within 30 days

Important Notes

  • Deletion cannot be undone
  • We recommend backing up your data before deletion
  • If we have legal retention obligations, some data may be retained in anonymized form
  • Deletion will take effect on all your devices and in the web application
